About

The lack of awareness surrounding the water crisis in households around the world is concerning. We aim to create awareness regarding the various ways that we can conserve water as it becomes a scarce resource for many around the world

Why has the conservation of water become necessary?

  • A child’s life is lost every 8 seconds due to the lack of clean, drinking water
  • By 2025 two-thirds of the world’s population will run out of fresh drinking water or be under stressed water condition
  • There are close to 844 million people without access to clean, drinking water in the world
  • Women are often tasked with the challenge of walking 5 to 10 kilometers to collect water
  • Due to the lack of access of water women and girls all around the world are forced to spend more than 200 M hours collecting water
  • Nearly 1 million people die each year from water, sanitation and hygiene-related diseases which could be reduced with access to safe water or sanitation
